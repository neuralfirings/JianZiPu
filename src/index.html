Preface with : and split characters with , or each character/letter will be split in its own line. You can use any characters in the standalone character table above, but the font is optimized to display vertically for: 1-10 (一到十), 0 (散), wai (外), shang (上), xia (下), gun (滚), fu (沸), dao (到), ru (如).
